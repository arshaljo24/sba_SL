Syllabus for CLASS -3

Skill-Based

Language:
    - Advanced storytelling and creative writing activities.
    - Focus on improved comprehension and communication skills.
Mathematics:
    - Progression to more complex concepts like geometry, measurement, and logical reasoning.
    - Application of mathematical skills to real-world problems.
Social Studies:
    - Exploration of community dynamics, economics, geography, history, and government structures.
    - Understanding of global issues and their impact on society.
Integrative Activities:
    - Project-based learning to encourage collaboration and critical thinking.
    - Hands-on experiences to enhance understanding of abstract concepts.
    - Field trips for experiential learning and exposure to real-world environments.
Overall Focus:
    - Development of creativity, effective communication, and a broader worldview.
    - Establishment of a solid foundation for future academic success and lifelong learning.

SKILL-BASED SYLLABUS

Concept-Based

- Class 3 marks a critical stage in primary education.
- Students tackle increasingly complex concepts in subjects like Maths, EVS, and English.
- Mastery of these subjects is crucial for overall academic success.
- Proficiency in Maths forms the basis for advanced mathematical learning.
- EVS covers topics like ecology and conservation, promoting environmental awareness.
- English skills are developed through reading, writing, and comprehension.
