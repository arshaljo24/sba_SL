Syllabus for UKG

Skill-Based Learning

Here's a condensed version of the skill-based syllabus outline for UKG kids:

    Language and Literacy Skills:
      - Phonics Awareness: Introduce letter sounds through interactive activities.
      - Vocabulary Building: Expand vocabulary through storytelling and word games.
      - Reading Readiness: Develop pre-reading skills like recognizing sight words.
      - Writing Skills: Practice tracing letters and forming simple words.
      Mathematical Skills
      - Number Recognition: Identify and count numbers up to 100.
      - Basic Operations: Introduce addition and subtraction concepts.
      - Measurement and Geometry: Explore shapes, sizes, and measurements.
      - Patterns and Sequences: Recognize and create simple patterns.
      Social and Emotional Skills
      - Self-Awareness: Encourage expression of feelings and emotions.
      - Social Interaction: Promote cooperation and turn-taking in group activities.
      - Empathy and Respect: Teach understanding and respect for differences.
      - Problem-Solving: Engage in simple critical thinking tasks.
      Creative Skills
      - Art and Craft: Allow for free art expression and creative activities.
      - Music and Movement: Explore rhythm and movement through songs and dances.
      - Dramatic Play: Encourage imaginative role-playing and storytelling.
      - Fine Motor Development: Develop fine motor skills through various activities.
      Physical Development
      - Gross Motor Skills: Practice running, jumping, and throwing.
      - Outdoor Play: Provide opportunities for outdoor exploration and play.
      - Health and Hygiene: Teach basic health habits like handwashing and healthy eating.
      Cognitive Skills
      - Critical Thinking: Stimulate logical thinking through puzzles and games.
      - Memory Skills: Play memory games to improve retention.
      - Attention and Concentration: Foster focus through engaging activities.
      - Curiosity and Exploration: Encourage exploration through hands-on experiences.
This skill-based syllabus aims to foster holistic development in UKG kids, preparing them for future academic challenges and lifelong learning.

SKILL-BASED SYLLABUS

Concept-Based Learning

    - **Language and Literacy:**
    - Letters, words, sentences
    - Storytelling, narratives
    - Basic grammar
    - Reading, writing skills
    - **Mathematics:**
    - Numbers, counting
    - Addition, subtraction
    - Shapes, properties
    - Measurement

    - **Science:**
    - Living, non-living things
    - Plants, animals, seasons, weather
    - Simple experiments
    - **Social Studies:**
    - Community, family concepts
    - Cultures, traditions
    - Basic geography
    - Citizenship, responsibility
    - **Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving:**
    - Problem-solving, decision-making
    - Logical thinking activities
    - Creativity, innovation
    - Cause and effect concepts
    Concept-based learning in UKG fosters a deeper understanding of fundamental concepts and critical thinking skills essential for lifelong learning.
