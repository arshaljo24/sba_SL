Syllabus for CLASS -3

Skill-Based

Class 3 students engage in skill-based learning with a holistic focus on language, mathematics, and social studies. Language activities include advanced storytelling and creative writing for improved comprehension and communication. In mathematics, students progress to more complex concepts like geometry, measurement, and logical reasoning. Social studies covers community dynamics, economics, geography, history, and government structures. Integrative activities, such as project-based learning and hands-on experiences, encourage collaboration and critical thinking. Field trips enhance experiential learning. This approach not only imparts subject-specific knowledge but also fosters creativity, effective communication, and a broader worldview, establishing a solid foundation for future academic success and lifelong learning.
