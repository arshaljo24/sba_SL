Syllabus for CLASS -1

Skill-Based Learning

Skill-based learning for Class 1 students prioritizes fundamental abilities across various domains:

    1. Language
    2. Math
    3. Social-emotional development
    4. Creativity
    5. Physical activities

It fosters:
    - Active listening
    - Confident expression
    - Numerical understanding
    - Problem-solving
    - This approach equips students for:
    - Lifelong learning
    - Adaptability

Concept-Based Learning

    - Focuses on fundamental concepts.
    - Emphasizes understanding connections.
    - Introduces shapes, numbers, community, environment.
    - Involves hands-on activities, discussions.
    - Develops critical thinking.
    - Fosters appreciation for interconnected learning.
    - Lays a solid foundation for academic success.
