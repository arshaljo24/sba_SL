Syllabus for UKG

Skill-Based Learning

Here's a condensed version of the skill-based syllabus outline for UKG kids:

    Language and Literacy Skills:
      1.Phonics Awareness:
      Introduce letter sounds through interactive activities.
      2.Vocabulary Building:
      Expand vocabulary through storytelling and word games.
      3.Reading Readiness:
      Develop pre-reading skills like recognizing sight words.
      4.Writing Skills:
      Practice tracing letters and forming simple words.

    Mathematical Skills
      1.Number Recognition:
      Identify and count numbers up to 100.
      2.Basic Operations:
      Introduce addition and subtraction concepts.
      3.Measurement and Geometry:
      Explore shapes, sizes, and measurements.
      4.Patterns and Sequences:
      Recognize and create simple patterns.

    Social and Emotional Skills
      1.Self-Awareness:
      Encourage expression of feelings and emotions.
      2.Social Interaction:
      Promote cooperation and turn-taking in group activities.
      3.Empathy and Respect:
      Teach understanding and respect for differences.
      4.Problem-Solving:
      Engage in simple critical thinking tasks.

    Creative Skills
      1.Art and Craft:
      Allow for free art expression and creative activities.
      2.Music and Movement:
      Explore rhythm and movement through songs and dances.
      3.Dramatic Play:
      Encourage imaginative role-playing and storytelling.
      4.Fine Motor Development:
      Develop fine motor skills through various activities.

    Physical Development

      1.Gross Motor Skills:
      Practice running, jumping, and throwing.
      2.Outdoor Play:
      Provide opportunities for outdoor exploration and play.
      3.Health and Hygiene:
      Teach basic health habits like handwashing and healthy eating.

    Cognitive Skills

      1.Critical Thinking:
      Stimulate logical thinking through puzzles and games.
      2.Memory Skills:
      Play memory games to improve retention.
      3.Attention and Concentration:
      Foster focus through engaging activities.
      4.Curiosity and Exploration:
      Encourage exploration through hands-on experiences.

This skill-based syllabus aims to foster holistic development in UKG kids, preparing them for future academic challenges and lifelong learning.

SKILL-BASED SYLLABUS

Concept-Based Learning

    Language and Literacy:
     - Letters, words, sentences
    - Storytelling, narratives
    - Basic grammar
    - Reading, writing skills

    Mathematics:
     - Numbers, counting
    - Addition, subtraction
    - Shapes, properties
    - Measurement

    Science:
     - Living, non-living things
    - Plants, animals, seasons, weather
    - Simple experiments

    Social Studies:
    - Community, family concepts
    - Cultures, traditions
    - Basic geography
    - Citizenship, responsibility

    Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving:
    - Problem-solving, decision-making
    - Logical thinking activities
    - Creativity, innovation
    - Cause and effect concepts

    Concept-based learning in UKG fosters a deeper understanding of fundamental concepts and critical thinking skills essential for lifelong learning.
